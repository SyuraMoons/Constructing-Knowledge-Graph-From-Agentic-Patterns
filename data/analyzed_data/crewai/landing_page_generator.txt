Web Design Crew Pattern
Deskripsi
Pattern ini implements landing page generation crew dengan UX researcher, copywriter, dan web designer agents. Crew creates complete landing pages from product concept termasuk hero section, features, testimonials, dan CTAs. Demonstrates multi-specialist collaboration untuk complex output.
